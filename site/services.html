Our Services

End-to-end consulting solutions — from research and evaluation to strategy, resource mobilization, and digital transformation.

Service 01

Research & Insight Generation

Research at BCL is a bridge between lived realities and informed action.

We design mixed-methods studies combining quantitative surveys with qualitative depth. Every engagement begins with participatory co-design sessions where clients, community representatives, and implementing partners help shape the questions that matter most.

Data is collected ethically and securely using digital platforms (KoboToolbox, SurveyCTO, ODK) with real-time geo-tagged entries. We triangulate findings through thematic coding (NVivo) and statistical analysis (R, SPSS, Stata). Validation workshops bring preliminary findings back to communities for feedback.

Our Research Services Include:

  • • Baseline, midline, and endline studies
  • • Needs assessments and feasibility studies
  • • Impact evaluations
  • • Policy and governance research
  • • Market and livelihood assessments
  • • Gender and social inclusion analysis
  • • Learning reviews and organizational assessments
Service 02

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)

MEL at BCL turns raw data into adaptive intelligence that donors trust and programmes thrive on.

We begin every engagement by facilitating theory-of-change workshops that align indicators with both donor requirements and on-the-ground realities. Progress is tracked through digital real-time monitoring dashboards (DHIS2, Power BI, Tableau), periodic field verifications, and third-party spot-checks.

Mid-term reviews and endline assessments draw on outcome harvesting, most significant change storytelling, and OECD-DAC criteria to capture not only what changed, but why and for whom.

Outcome: Partners leave with stronger adaptive capacity, credible evidence for funding renewal, and practical lessons that make future programming more effective and equitable.

Request This Service

Our MEL Expertise Includes:

  • • MEL framework design and indicator tracking
  • • Baseline, mid-term, and endline evaluations
  • • Impact assessments
  • • Data quality assessments
  • • Real-time dashboards and reporting systems
  • • Learning workshops and reflection sessions
Service 03

Strategic Planning & Organizational Development

Strategic planning at BCL transforms organizational ambition into a living, owned roadmap resilient to donor shifts and external shocks.

We start with diagnostic conversations — stakeholder interviews, environmental scans, and SWOT analyses — to map current realities and collective aspirations. Facilitated workshops guide teams to articulate robust theories of change, weaving in gender, youth, climate, and equity lenses.

Scenario planning exercises test pathways against possible futures (funding cuts, climate events, policy changes), while prioritization tools ensure focus on high-impact interventions.

Request This Service

Our Strategic Planning Services:

  • • Strategic plan development
  • • Organizational assessments and SWOT analysis
  • • Theory of Change facilitation
  • • Risk analysis and scenario planning
  • • Operational planning and governance strengthening
  • • Institutional capacity assessments
Service 04

Resource Mobilization & Proposal Development

In an era of donor fatigue and intense competition, funding strategy at BCL builds financial resilience.

We begin with comprehensive donor landscape mapping — identifying aligned bilateral, multilateral, philanthropic, and private-sector opportunities, including innovative finance mechanisms. Organizational readiness is assessed, and gaps are closed so eligibility barriers are removed early.

When calls arise, we lead or co-lead the development of compelling, evidence-rich concept notes, full proposals, and budgets — always emphasizing measurable impact, value-for-money, and scalable pathways.

Request This Service

Our Resource Mobilization Services:

  • • Donor landscape mapping
  • • Proposal development and grant writing
  • • Fundraising and partnership strategy
  • • Diversification strategies (CSR, diaspora philanthropy)
  • • Long-term donor stewardship plans
Service 05

GIS Mapping & Spatial Analytics

Spatial and digital data tools at BCL turn location into leverage — bringing precision, visibility, and equity to programmes.

Using ArcGIS, QGIS, and Google Earth Engine, we layer satellite imagery, administrative boundaries, and community-collected data to create custom maps and hotspot analyses. Digital survey tools with built-in geo-tagging (ODK, SurveyCTO) ensure field data is accurate and instantly mappable.

Outcome: Sharper targeting, reduced wastage, more equitable reach, and compelling visual evidence that strengthens proposals and reporting.

Request This Service

Our Geospatial Services:

  • • GIS mapping and spatial data analysis
  • • Geo-tagged surveys
  • • Satellite imagery analysis
  • • Interactive dashboards
  • • Spatial planning support
  • • Programme visualization tools
Service 06

Policy Influence & Governance Support

Policy influence and governance support at BCL uses evidence as a lever to shift systems and create enabling environments for scaled, sustainable change.

We synthesize rigorous research to pinpoint policy gaps, implementation barriers, and high-leverage entry points. Governance diagnostics assess institutional structures, decision-making flows, financial management, and capacity constraints.

From diagnostics, we co-create advocacy strategies — stakeholder power maps, influence pathways, messaging frameworks, and coalition-building plans — designed to move key decision-makers.

Service 07

Digital Transformation & Responsible AI

Digital transformation and AI integration at BCL is handled with deliberate responsibility — technology amplifies human insight and community voice, never replaces it.

We begin with a participatory digital maturity assessment to map current systems, skill levels, pain points, and organizational ambitions. Solutions are chosen thoughtfully: low/no-code platforms and ethical AI applications that integrate seamlessly without demanding heavy infrastructure.

We align with the African Union's Continental AI Strategy and emerging data sovereignty principles, ensuring all implementations remain inclusive, ethical, and context-sensitive.

  • • Digital literacy and responsible AI training
