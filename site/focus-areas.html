Focus Areas

BCL's work spans Africa's most pressing development priorities. Our multidisciplinary structure enables integrated, cross-sectoral solutions.

Area 01

Education & Human Development

We support inclusive education systems, skills development, learning equity, teacher support, education strategy, and youth empowerment programmes.

Our education work spans curriculum development, teacher professional development, learning assessments, education in emergencies, and system strengthening. We combine rigorous research with practical implementation support to help governments, NGOs, and foundations deliver quality education for all.

Discuss an Education Project

Key Capabilities

  • → Baseline, midline & endline education studies
  • → Curriculum and pedagogy reviews
  • → Teacher professional development design
  • → Education in emergencies & conflict contexts
  • → Learning outcome measurement frameworks
Area 02

Gender Equality & Women's Empowerment

We design and evaluate programmes that promote gender justice, women's leadership, GBV prevention, social inclusion, and economic empowerment.

Our gender work is intersectional and context-sensitive. We conduct gender analyses, develop gender strategies, design gender-transformative programmes, and evaluate gender outcomes using feminist evaluation approaches.

Discuss a Gender Project

Key Capabilities

  • → Gender analysis and mainstreaming
  • → GBV prevention programme design
  • → Women's leadership and participation
  • → Feminist evaluation approaches
  • → Social inclusion and intersectionality
Area 03

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)

We help organizations track results, measure impact, strengthen accountability, and embed adaptive learning into programming.

Our MEL practice is built on the principle that evaluation should be useful, not just compliant. We design frameworks that serve programme teams as much as donors — generating real-time insights that inform adaptive management and strengthen programme quality.

Discuss MEL Support

Key Capabilities

  • → Results frameworks and indicator design
  • → Real-time monitoring dashboards
  • → Outcome harvesting and MSC
  • → OECD-DAC evaluation criteria
  • → Learning and reflection facilitation
Area 04

Peacebuilding, Governance & Community Resilience

Our work supports civic engagement, social cohesion, conflict-sensitive programming, governance strengthening, and resilience building.

We work in fragile and conflict-affected contexts, applying conflict-sensitive approaches to programme design, implementation, and evaluation. Our governance work strengthens local institutions, promotes accountability, and builds community resilience to shocks.

Discuss Peacebuilding

Key Capabilities

  • → Conflict analysis and sensitivity assessments
  • → Social cohesion programming
  • → Civic engagement and participation
  • → Governance and institutional strengthening
  • → Community resilience and DRR

We conduct policy reviews, governance assessments, stakeholder mapping, and evidence-based advocacy support.

Our policy work bridges research and action. We translate complex findings into accessible policy briefs, facilitate multi-stakeholder dialogues, and support organizations to build advocacy strategies that shift decision-making at local, national, and regional levels.

Environmental Protection, Agriculture & Food Security

We promote climate-smart agriculture, sustainable livelihoods, food systems strengthening, and environmental resilience.

Our environmental and agriculture work integrates GIS mapping, value chain analysis, and participatory research to design programmes that are both productive and sustainable. We prioritize smallholder inclusion and equitable benefit sharing.

Discuss Agriculture & Environment

Key Capabilities

  • → Value chain and market analysis
  • → Food systems and nutrition assessments
  • → Environmental and social impact assessments

Public & Community Health / WASH

We support health systems strengthening, WASH programming, public health research, community wellbeing, and integrated health interventions.

Our health practice spans preventive and curative services, with a strong emphasis on community-based approaches that reach the most vulnerable. We integrate WASH, nutrition, and health systems thinking into every engagement.

Youth Empowerment & Local Economic Development

We help organizations design sustainable livelihood programmes, entrepreneurship initiatives, skills development, and youth participation strategies.

Africa's youth demographic is both an opportunity and a challenge. We work with partners to build pathways from education to employment, support youth-led enterprises, and create platforms for meaningful youth civic participation.

Climate Resilience, Adaptation & Climate Finance

We support organizations to access climate finance, strengthen adaptation programming, integrate resilience approaches, and implement climate-smart initiatives.

Climate change intensifies vulnerabilities across Africa. We bridge the gap between climate finance mechanisms and on-the-ground implementation — helping organizations navigate GCF, AfDB, and Adaptation Fund processes while ensuring locally-led adaptation.

Discuss Climate Resilience

Key Capabilities

  • → Climate vulnerability and risk assessments
  • → GCF, AfDB and Adaptation Fund proposals
  • → NDC alignment and national climate strategies
  • → Climate MEL systems
  • → Early-warning and spatial modelling

Digital Transformation & Responsible AI

We help organizations adopt ethical, practical, and context-sensitive digital solutions — from AI-supported analytics to real-time dashboards and GIS integration.

We align with the African Union's Continental AI Strategy and emerging data sovereignty principles, ensuring all implementations remain inclusive, ethical, and context-sensitive. Technology amplifies human insight — never replaces it.
