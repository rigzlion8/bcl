About Us

We are a Nairobi-based, African-led development consulting firm delivering practical, evidence-driven solutions since 2018.

Who We Are

Evidence-Driven. Africa-Led. Impact-Focused.

BCL partners with NGOs, UN agencies, governments, foundations, and private-sector actors across Eastern, Western, Southern, and Central Africa.

Basal Consultancy Limited (BCL) is a Nairobi-based development consulting firm established in 2018. We provide practical, evidence-driven solutions that strengthen organizations, empower communities, and advance equitable development across Africa.

With a core team and a flexible pool of over 50 consultants plus a growing pan-African associate network, we combine technical excellence, deep contextual knowledge, and practical innovation to help organizations achieve measurable, lasting impact.

Our work spans Eastern, Western, Southern, and Central Africa, supporting development actors to design better programmes, improve accountability, strengthen systems, and achieve measurable impact.

We specialize in:

  • → Research & Insight Generation
  • → Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)
  • → Strategic Planning & Organizational Development
  • → Resource Mobilization & Proposal Development
  • → Climate Resilience & Climate Finance Advisory
  • → Digital Transformation & Responsible AI
  • → GIS Mapping & Spatial Analytics
  • → Capacity Strengthening & Technical Advisory
